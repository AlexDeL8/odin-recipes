Seared Steak

Description

A classic take on a classic comfort dish

This is a simple seared steak recipe that can be altered any way you'd like to match your taste

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Fresh Herbs

Steps

  1. Put butter in high heat pan
  2. Lay steak down and season top side with salt and pepper, each side (2-3 minutes)
  3. Lower heat after and add more butter, garlic, and fresh herbs
  4. Baste for 2 minutes, remove steak, let rest for 5 minutes
