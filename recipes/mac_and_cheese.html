Mac and Cheese

Description

A classic take on a classic comfort dish

This is a simple base mac and cheese recipe that can be altered any way you'd like to match your taste

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil pasta just undercooked
  2. Make a roux from butter, flour, and milk
  3. Mix in cheese to the roux, until melted
  4. Add slightly undercooked pasta to cheese sauce, season to taste
