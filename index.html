Mac and Cheese

Description

A classic take on a classic comfort dish

This is a simple base mac and cheese recipe that can be altered any way you'd like to match your taste

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil pasta just undercooked
  2. Make a roux from butter, flour, and milk
  3. Mix in cheese to the roux, until melted
  4. Add slightly undercooked pasta to cheese sauce, season to taste

Seared Steak

Description

A classic take on a classic comfort dish

This is a simple seared steak recipe that can be altered any way you'd like to match your taste

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Fresh Herbs

Steps

  1. Put butter in high heat pan
  2. Lay steak down and season top side with salt and pepper, each side (2-3 minutes)
  3. Lower heat after and add more butter, garlic, and fresh herbs
  4. Baste for 2 minutes, remove steak, let rest for 5 minutes

Crispy Potatoes

Description

A classic take on a classic comfort dish

This is a simple crispy potatoes recipe that can be altered any way you'd like to match your taste

Ingredients

  • 3 Idaho Potatoes
  • Olive OIl
  • Old Bay Seasoning
  • Vinegar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 500F
  2. Cube potatoes, cover and boil with 1 inch extra cold water in pot with splash vinegar
  3. Strain potatoes when can slightly pierce with fork
  4. Put potatoes in baking tray, with olive oil and seasoning to coat
  5. Bake for 35 minutes
